About me...

Coming from a family of army men, a boy with patriotic thoughts and techy interests, who want to live life like iron man and believe in love and respect for women. Hi myself Abhishek Singh, and this is my brief introduction, as I am not much good at writing but still trying my best...

I am originally from Basti, Uttarpradesh, but ss my father is in BSF, I had spent my early childhood in Jaiselmer, Rajasthan, and childhood in Tripura. Where Jaiselmer a prosperous city to Agartala a place where I had seen extreme poverty, some people who come to ous quarters as labours and kamwali, just take meal once in a day.
